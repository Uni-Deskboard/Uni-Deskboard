Uni-Deskboard

Uni-Deskboard is an intelligent platform that let you navigate faster and with ease between all the university apps.

Never remember a password again

All your usernames and passwords are safely memorized and automaticaly entered when you access an app. You'll never have to remember all those passwords again.

Instant notifications

Never miss a deadline. With Uni-Deskboard, you'll get a notification every time something is updated on one of the apps and when a deadline is approaching. The notifications are fully customizable.

Apps supported

MonLabXL, MapleTA, Blackboard, gmail, echo360 and uozone are all accessible from Uni-Deskboard.

Mobile app

Uni-Deskboard is also available as a mobile app. Notifcations and apps support optimised for mobile

Fully costumizable

Organize your app the way you want it. Get the notifications you want, at the time you want. Adapt the app to how you work, not the other way around.

Quicker access

You'll never navigate faster: with one click you get where you want to go. Forget about the loading time so you can focus on your work
